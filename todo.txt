Good work on V2.

Now we move from MVP to Product-Level Architecture.

Current system works technically, but now it needs to become a real client-ready AI Follow-up Agent product.

Your next task is to work on “Multi-Client + Company Knowledge + Better Lead Qualification”.

---

Objective:

Make the system usable for multiple businesses, where each business can have its own:

* company details
* services
* qualification questions
* follow-up style
* lead handling rules

Instead of one fixed system for everyone.

---

Step 1: Company Profile Table

Create a new table:

company_profiles

Fields:

* id
* company_name
* industry
* services (JSON/text)
* intro_message
* qualification_questions (JSON)
* pricing_notes
* preferred_channel (email / whatsapp / call)
* created_at

This should act as the “brain” of the AI agent for each company.

---
 Step 2: Link Leads to Company

Update leads table:

Add:

* company_id (foreign key)

Every lead must belong to one company.

This is required for multi-client support.

---

 Step 3: Dynamic Message Generation

Current emails are generic.

Update system so messages are generated using:

* company intro
* services
* lead requirement
* qualification questions

Example:

“Hi John, this is from Coirei. We help businesses with AI solutions and web development. You mentioned you are looking for a web app. Can you share more details about your requirement?”

Do not hardcode messages.

Use DB/JSON driven responses.

---

Step 4: Better Lead Qualification

Current scoring is basic.

Improve scoring using:

* budget clarity
* timeline urgency
* seriousness of requirement
* response speed
* reply quality
* repeated engagement

Classification:

* Good Lead
* Maybe
* Not Interested

Store detailed reason also.

Example:
“Good Lead because budget mentioned + urgent timeline + clear requirement”

Not just score.

---

 Step 5: Follow-up Rules Per Company

Different companies need different follow-ups.

Example:

Education:

* fee reminders
* installment payment reminders

Service Business:

* proposal follow-up
* sales qualification

Create configurable follow-up rules based on company type.

Do not keep one static flow.

---

Step 6: Dashboard Upgrade

Dashboard should show:

* company-wise leads
* classification summary
* hot leads first
* follow-up pending leads
* conversion-focused view

Think from business owner perspective, not developer perspective.

---

Step 7: Prepare for WhatsApp Integration

Even if not fully implemented:

Create architecture so WhatsApp can be plugged in easily later.

Do not tightly couple everything only to Gmail.

Need scalable structure.

---

 Expected Output:

* Updated DB schema
* Dynamic company-driven messages
* Better qualification logic
* Improved dashboard structure
* Cleaner product architecture

---

Important:

Do not overcomplicate with unnecessary AI.

Focus on:
structure + scalability + business usability
This should start looking like a real SaaS product, not just a technical demo.
